I obtained the Ph.D. degree in the department of Computer Science & Engineering, Hong Kong University of Science and Technology in 2019. My Ph.D. supervisor was Prof. Pan Hui. Before that, I received my B.E. degree from School of Electronic Science and Engineering, Nanjing University in 2014. In September 2019, I start my job as a Senior Engineer in Hong Kong Applied Science And Technology Research Institute.

Research Interest

My research interest lies at the intersection of mobile computing and computer vision.
I like designing and building intelligent mobile systems that integrates mobile computing and computer vision techniques, to address real-life problems and assist human in daily activities.
My current research topics include visual privacy protection, augmented reality, and emotion sensing.
